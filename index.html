eagle

Eagles are admired the world over as living symbols of power, freedom, and transcendence.

  • Scientific Name: Accipitridae
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Maximum Speed: 160 km/h
  • Sight Distance: up to 3.2 km

In First World civilizations, the lifestyle of an eagle hunter is unwelcome. Amazingly, though, the eagle is the perfect solution for an advanced technological threat: drones. With the ubiquity and accessibility of drones in the modern world, not all tech enthusiasts are using them for recreational purposes. These drones can be used to covertly spy on buildings for later theft as well as to drop explosives.
